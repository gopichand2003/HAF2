Statement of Faith

Our core beliefs and values

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God, serving as our ultimate guide for faith and practice.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the presence and power of the Holy Spirit, who guides, comforts, and empowers believers.

Salvation

We believe salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ and His sacrificial death and resurrection.

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and service.

Service

We believe in serving others as an expression of God's love and our faith in action.

Love

We believe in demonstrating God's love through compassion, acceptance, and care for all people.
